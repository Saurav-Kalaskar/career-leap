# Mode: contact - LinkedIn Power Move

1. Identify targets via WebSearch:
   - Hiring manager
   - Assigned recruiter
   - 2-3 peer engineers
   - Interviewer (if interview already scheduled)

2. Classify contact type:
   - Recruiter
   - Hiring Manager
   - Peer
   - Interviewer

3. Select primary target based on expected response probability and role relevance.

4. Generate a 3-sentence outreach message tailored by contact type.

### Recruiter
- Fit: direct role/experience/location alignment
- Proof: one screening-ready credential
- CTA: soft ask to share resume and discuss fit

### Hiring Manager
- Hook: specific team/product challenge
- Proof: strongest quantified relevant win
- CTA: interest in discussing approach to that challenge

### Peer
- Interest: reference their work/public content
- Connection: relevant overlap from your projects
- CTA: ask for perspective, not a direct job ask

### Interviewer
- Research: specific reference to their work
- Context: concise overlap with your experience
- CTA: looking forward to interview conversation

5. Provide EN by default (and localized variant only if explicitly requested).

## Message Rules

- Max 300 characters for connection requests.
- No generic corporate language.
- No weak filler.
- Show specific context + proof.
- Never share phone number in cold outreach.
